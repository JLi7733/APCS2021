Robotics

Robotics at Greenhill has two main teams. One being Motorhead and the other being Viridian

The robotics club participates in the FTC competition and has won many awards there including the Inspire Award along with making multiple appearances at the FTC Regional Competitions

The team is led by the one and only Mrs. Suarez!
